== Installation ==

### Download the SupportAI ZIP file.  
**Installing the ZIP File**  

1. Log in to your WordPress admin panel.   
2. Go to WordPress Plugins page and click on Add New button, then Upload the .zip file that you downloaded.
3. Then click "Install Now" and then "Activate"
4. Enter your API Key
5. Select the chatbot you want to integrate on your website
	
**Installing via WordPress Plugin Search**  

1. Log in to your WordPress admin panel.   
2. Go to WordPress Plugins page and search for "SupportAI"
3. Then click "Install Now" and then "Activate"
4. Enter your API Key
5. Select the chatbot you want to integrate on your website


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does it work? =

SupportAI creates custom chatbots trained with your data. There is nothing more frustrating as a business than a customer being left with unanswered questions. SupportAI solves this problem by providing a chatbot that can answer any question about your business in seconds.

= What can I use SupportAI for? =

SupportAI is incredibly versatile, perfect for a variety of needs including Customer Support, Lead Generation, Sales Bot, ChatGPT Integration, Internal Employee Support, Automated Booking Scheduling, Feedback Collection, Product Recommendations, Learning, and much more!

= How can I integrate SupportAI on my website? =

To integrate a chatbot using SupportAI, start by entering your unique API key on the platform. Once authenticated, you'll be presented with a dashboard showcasing all your created chatbots. Locate the chatbot you wish to integrate and click on the "Integrate" button next to its name. Following this action, navigate to your website, and you should observe the chatbot bubble automatically appearing, signaling successful integration. This bubble is your direct link to the chatbot for your site visitors, offering them instant assistance or engagement as configured.
